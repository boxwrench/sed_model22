# SECTION 3: PROCESS FLOW DESCRIPTION

## Process Overview

```
Raw Water (Source A or Source B)
    ↓
Chemical Addition (Caustic Soda, CO₂, Hypochlorite, Polymer, Alum)
    ↓
Flash Mix Vault
    ↓
Flow Distribution Structure
    ↓
Flocculation Basins (4 units)
    ↓
Sedimentation Basins (4 units) → Sludge to Lagoons 3-7
    ↓
Filters (12 units) → Backwash to Recovery System
    ↓
Clear Well (324,000 gal)
    ↓
Final Chemical Addition (Bisulfite, Caustic, Hypochlorite)
    ↓
Effluent to Distribution System / Utility Water System
```

|--------|-------------|
| Source A | Primary raw water source |
| Source B | Secondary raw water source |

### Flash Mix Vault
- Initial rapid mixing chamber for coagulant dispersion
- Emergency overflow pathway included
- Chemical feed connections: Sodium Hypochlorite, Cationic Polymer, Alum/Ferric Chloride

- Receives flow from Flash Mix Vault

- Emergency overflow connection

# SECTION 7: DESIGN NOTES

1. **G-VALUES** listed are applicable both at 80 and 160 M.G.D. plant flow rates.

2. **MIXING ENERGY**: The energy for mixing is supplied from an external source and is independent of plant flow.

3. **CARBON DIOXIDE** is added by sidestream injection to raw water.

4. **CHEMICAL ADDITION LOCATIONS**: Certain chemicals can be added through the settled water distribution panel via flash mix pumps or at weir troughs.

5. **POTASSIUM PERMANGANATE**: Pre-oxidation capability provided at raw water intake.
