## Notes

1. **CARBON DIOXIDE** is added by sidestream injection to raw water

2. **CHEMICAL ADDITION LOCATIONS**: These chemicals can be added through the settled water distribution panel via flash mix pumps or at weir troughs

3. **POTASSIUM PERMANGANATE**: The ability to add potassium permanganate upstream of the plant is being addressed in another contract

---

## Process Flow Summary

```
Raw Water (SA/HH or Calaveras)
    ↓
Chemical Addition (Caustic Soda, CO₂, Hypochlorite, Polymer, Alum)
    ↓
Flash Mix Vault
    ↓
Flow Distribution Structure
    ↓
Flocculation Basins (4 units)
    ↓
Sedimentation Basins (4 units) → Sludge to Lagoons 3-7
    ↓
Filters (12 units) → Backwash to Recovery System
    ↓
Clear Well (324,000 gal)
    ↓
Final Chemical Addition (Bisulfite, Caustic, Hypochlorite)
    ↓
Effluent to HH Aqueduct / Utility Water System
```
